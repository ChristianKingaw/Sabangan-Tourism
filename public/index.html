FEEL THE BREEZE, WALK IN THE CLOUDS

Brace yourself for a short yet captivating trail experience for this years’ 2026 Gagayam Festival. With a mix of pine forest, mossy trails and grassland, the Gagayam Trail Experience will provide mix emotions and memorable experience.

Along the trails, you will have a glimpse birds eye view of the locality’s rich landscapes and the community. Another challenging yet attainable experience to all trail enthusiast.
