Festival Introduction

Welcome to the GAGAYAM FESTIVAL

Gagayam Festival celebrates Sabangan's mountain culture through outdoor adventure, local artistry, and community gatherings that bring visitors and residents together.

From scenic trail routes to cultural showcases, every activity highlights the highland spirit and invites everyone to experience the beauty of Sabangan.
